Zhengwentai SUN

I am Zhengwentai SUN (孙正文泰), a second-year MPhil student at Hong Kong Polytechnic University. I completed my Bachelor's degree in Automation Engineering at the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China. My current research focuses on generative models and their applications in the fashion domain, under the supervision of Prof. Tracy Mok.
